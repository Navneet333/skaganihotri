MORE ABOUT PUJA'S

Types of
Puja

01

Griha Shanti Puja

Performed to bring peace, prosperity, and harmony to your home. This puja mitigates the effects of negative energies and creates a sanctified atmosphere, promoting well-being and happiness.

Contact Us
02

Vastu Shanti Puja

A ritual to purify and correct the Vastu of your home or office. It ensures that the physical space is aligned with positive energies, bringing good fortune and success to all occupants.

Contact Us
03

Mangal Shanti Puja

This puja is performed to appease the planet Mars and remove any Mangal Dosha in the horoscope. It helps in overcoming obstacles in marriage, health issues, and financial challenges.

Contact Us
04

Kaal Sarpa Dosh Puja

A special ritual to mitigate the effects of Kaal Sarpa Dosha in one’s horoscope. This puja is believed to remove obstacles, bring peace, and improve overall well-being and success in life.

Contact Us
05

Janam Patrika

Get an in-depth analysis of your birth chart with Janam Patrika. This service offers personalized insights into your life’s journey, helping you make informed decisions for a better future.

Contact Us
06

Yajna Prana Pratishtha

A sacred ritual that involves invoking divine energies into an idol or deity during a temple installation. This puja purifies the space and ensures the deity’s blessings for the devotees

Contact Us
07

Muhurat Vichar

Selecting the most auspicious time for important events in your life, such as weddings, housewarmings, or business ventures. Muhurat Vichar ensures success and prosperity in your endeavors.

Contact Us
08

Navagraha Puja

A comprehensive ritual to appease the nine planets (Navagrahas) and mitigate their adverse effects. This puja is performed to bring balance, peace, and positive energy into your life

Contact Us
09

Rudrabhishek Puja

A powerful ritual dedicated to Lord Shiva, performed with the chanting of Vedic mantras. Rudrabhishek is known to purify the mind, body, and soul, bringing prosperity and divine blessings.

Contact Us
10

Satyanarayan Puja

A popular and highly revered puja dedicated to Lord Vishnu, performed to seek blessings for health, wealth, and prosperity. This puja is often done on special occasions and family gatherings.

Contact Us
11

Malik Kariya

All types of Malik Kariya are performed, addressing spiritual and religious needs. These rituals, led by experienced priests, ensure the removal of obstacles and promote the well-being and prosperity of individuals and families.
